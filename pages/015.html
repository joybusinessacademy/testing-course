Palletised freight can be loaded onto trucks as either a FTL or LTL.

Click on each truck to find out more.

FTL or “Full truckload” is where one customer fills the truck with one consignment of goods.

As mentioned earlier Full Truck Loads will normally be delivered directly from origin to end destination which saves time and effort throughout the Transport process.

 LTL or “Less than Truckload” is used where multiple customers use multiple consignments to fill the trucks capacity. Loads then are transported to a Mainfreight Branch for sorting to ensure the correct items get delivered to the end destination.
